About Us

A Legacy of Knowledge & Character

Gyan Mandir Gurukul is a respected institution in the heart of Ahmedabad that blends the timeless wisdom of India's ancient Gurukul tradition with the rigour of modern education. We believe that true education shapes not just the mind, but the soul.

Our students grow into confident, compassionate, and capable individuals ready to lead tomorrow's world with integrity and purpose.

Our Vision

  • To develop strong moral character and ethical values
  • To create future leaders who serve society with humility
  • To preserve and promote India's rich cultural heritage
